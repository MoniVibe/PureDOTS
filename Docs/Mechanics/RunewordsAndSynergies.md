# Mechanic: Runewords, Enchantment Combos & Addon Synergies

## Overview

**Status**: Concept
**Complexity**: Very Complex
**Category**: Itemization / Crafting / Progression

**One-line description**: *Diablo 2-style socket systems where items/modules can be combined to form powerful runewords, enchantments can synergize for bonus effects, consumables combo for unique benefits, and augments create set bonuses.*

## Core Concept

Items, modules, potions, and augments are more than simple stat sticks - they form a **combinatorial puzzle** where specific combinations unlock hidden bonuses:

1. **Runewords (Equipment/Modules)**: Socket items with runes/cores in specific orders to unlock named runeword bonuses
2. **Enchantment Synergies**: Certain enchantments on same item trigger combo effects
3. **Consumable Combos**: Taking multiple potions/elixirs simultaneously unlocks unique buffs
4. **Augment Set Bonuses**: Equipping augments from same manufacturer/set grants escalating bonuses

This system rewards **build creativity**, **theorycrafting**, and **long-term planning**. Players hunt specific runes, enchantments, and augments to complete powerful combinations.

## How It Works

### 1. Runewords (Socket System)

#### Godgame: Equipment Sockets & Runes

**Equipment with Sockets**:
- Weapons, armor, tools can have **sockets** (1-6 sockets, rare items have more)
- Sockets are empty slots where **runes** can be inserted
- Runes are special items found as loot, each with unique letter/symbol (e.g., **Rune: Tir**, **Rune: El**)

**Runeword Formation**:
- Insert runes in specific order to form **runeword**
- Example: Inserting **Tir + El + Eth** (in that order) into 3-socket weapon creates **"Steel"** runeword
- Runeword grants bonus beyond individual rune effects

**Example Runewords (Godgame)**:

| Runeword | Runes Required | Item Type | Effects |
|----------|---------------|-----------|---------|
| **Steel** | Tir + El + Eth | Weapon (3 sockets) | +30% damage, +25% attack speed |
| **Stealth** | Tal + Eth | Armor (2 sockets) | +25% movement speed, -15% visibility |
| **Ancient's Pledge** | Ral + Ort + Tal | Shield (3 sockets) | +50% fire resist, +50% cold resist, +50% lightning resist |
| **Leaf** | Tir + Ral | Staff (2 sockets) | +3 to fire skills, +33% fire damage |
| **Infinity** | Ber + Mal + Ber + Ist | Weapon (4 sockets) | -50% enemy defense, +35% chance to cast chain lightning on hit |

**Rune Scarcity**:
- Low-tier runes (Tir, El, Ral) are common drops
- Mid-tier runes (Tal, Ort, Eth) are uncommon
- High-tier runes (Ber, Mal, Ist, Jah, Lo) are extremely rare (legendary tier)
- Runewords with high-tier runes are endgame goals

#### Space4X: Module Sockets & Cores

**Modules with Sockets**:
- Ship modules (weapons, shields, engines) have **core sockets** (1-4 sockets)
- **Cores** are specialized components (e.g., **Flux Core**, **Plasma Core**, **Void Core**)

**Core Sequences**:
- Insert cores in specific order to activate **core sequence** bonus
- Example: **Flux + Plasma + Flux** in 3-socket weapon creates **"Trinity Cannon"** sequence
- Grants unique firing mode or massive stat boost

**Example Core Sequences (Space4X)**:

| Sequence | Cores Required | Module Type | Effects |
|----------|---------------|-------------|---------|
| **Trinity Cannon** | Flux + Plasma + Flux | Weapon (3 sockets) | +50% damage, fires 3-round burst |
| **Aegis Shield** | Barrier + Null + Barrier | Shield (3 sockets) | +100% shield capacity, absorbs debuffs |
| **Warp Engine** | Phase + Void + Phase | Engine (3 sockets) | +50% speed, can phase through asteroids |
| **Overmind Matrix** | Neural + Sync + Neural + Sync | Computer (4 sockets) | +3 to all officer skills, shared XP in fleet |

### 2. Enchantment Synergies

**Enchantment System**:
- Items can be **enchanted** with magical properties (Godgame) or **tech upgrades** (Space4X)
- Each item can hold multiple enchantments (typically 3-5)
- Certain enchantment **pairs or sets** synergize, granting bonus effects

#### Godgame: Enchantment Combos

**Example Synergies**:

| Enchantment 1 | Enchantment 2 | Synergy Bonus |
|--------------|--------------|---------------|
| **Flame** (+fire damage) | **Molten** (+burning duration) | **Inferno**: Enemies explode on death, spreading fire |
| **Ice** (+cold damage) | **Frost** (+slow) | **Deep Freeze**: Chance to freeze enemies solid (stun) |
| **Lightning** (+shock damage) | **Storm** (+chain) | **Thunderstrike**: Lightning chains to 2 additional targets |
| **Heal** (+heal potency) | **Regeneration** (+HP regen) | **Vitality**: Overheal becomes temporary max HP buffer |
| **Sharpness** (+damage) | **Precision** (+crit chance) | **Executioner**: Crits deal 3x damage instead of 2x |

**Tri-Synergies** (3+ enchantments):

| Enchantments | Tri-Synergy Bonus |
|-------------|------------------|
| **Flame + Ice + Lightning** | **Elemental Mastery**: All elemental damage +50%, chance to trigger random elemental explosion |
| **Heal + Protection + Regeneration** | **Divine Resilience**: Cannot be reduced below 1 HP (once per day) |
| **Sharpness + Speed + Precision** | **Bladestorm**: Attacks hit in small AoE, multiple targets per swing |

#### Space4X: Tech Upgrade Synergies

**Example Synergies**:

| Upgrade 1 | Upgrade 2 | Synergy Bonus |
|----------|----------|---------------|
| **Overclocked Reactor** (+power) | **Cooling Array** (+heat dissipation) | **Fusion Surge**: 20% chance on hit to fire bonus shot (free) |
| **Hardened Hull** (+armor) | **Ablative Coating** (+damage reduction) | **Fortress**: Damage reduction applies to shields as well |
| **Targeting Matrix** (+accuracy) | **Predictive AI** (+tracking) | **Perfect Shot**: First shot in combat is guaranteed crit |
| **Stealth Baffles** (+evasion) | **ECM Suite** (+jamming) | **Ghost**: Completely invisible to sensors if not firing |

### 3. Consumable Combos (Potions, Elixirs, Stims)

**Consumable System**:
- Entities can consume **potions** (Godgame) or **stims/elixirs** (Space4X)
- Normally, potions provide single benefit (heal, buff, resist)
- Taking **multiple potions simultaneously** (within same turn/action) triggers **combo effects**

#### Godgame: Potion Combos

**Example Combos**:

| Potion 1 | Potion 2 | Combo Effect |
|----------|----------|--------------|
| **Health Potion** | **Stamina Potion** | **Vitality Surge**: +50% max HP for 10 minutes |
| **Strength Potion** | **Agility Potion** | **Athlete's Grace**: +focus, +jump height, +dodge (athleticism checks) |
| **Fire Resist** | **Cold Resist** | **Elemental Balance**: +flat damage resistance (all types) |
| **Fire Resist** | **Cold Resist** | **Lightning Resist** | **Tri-Resist**: Immune to all elemental damage for 30 seconds |
| **Mana Potion** | **Intelligence Potion** | **Arcane Overflow**: Spells cost -50% mana for 5 minutes |

**Alchemy Mastery**:
- Skilled alchemists can **pre-brew combo potions** (single potion with combo effect built-in)
- Expensive but convenient (no need to chug 3 potions mid-combat)

#### Space4X: Stim/Enhancement Combos

**Example Combos**:

| Stim 1 | Stim 2 | Combo Effect |
|--------|--------|--------------|
| **Combat Stim** (+reaction) | **Focus Enhancer** (+accuracy) | **Battle Trance**: Crew dodges 50% of incoming fire |
| **Repair Nanites** (heal) | **Overcharge** (+power) | **Emergency Protocols**: Ship auto-repairs critical systems during combat |
| **Radiation Shielding** | **Toxin Filter** | **Bio-Hazard Suit**: Crew immune to environmental damage (gas, radiation, etc.) |

### 4. Augment Set Bonuses (Cyberware, Limb Replacements)

**Augment System**:
- Entities can equip **augments** (cybernetic limbs, enhancements, implants)
- Augments from **same manufacturer** or **same set** grant escalating bonuses

#### Godgame: Magical Augments (Prosthetics, Enchanted Limbs)

**Example Sets**:

| Set Name | Manufacturer | Augments in Set | Set Bonuses |
|----------|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| **Ironclad** | Dwarven Forge | Iron Arm, Iron Leg, Iron Heart, Iron Skull | (2) +20 armor<br>(3) +30% melee damage<br>(4) Immune to stagger/knockback |
| **Shadowstep** | Assassin's Guild | Shadow Hand, Shadow Foot, Shadow Eye | (2) +25% stealth<br>(3) Can teleport short distance (1/day) |
| **Arcane Conduit** | Mage Circle | Arcane Brain, Arcane Heart, Arcane Eye | (2) +30% spell damage<br>(3) Spells cost -25% mana |

**Mix-and-Match**:
- Entity can mix sets (e.g., 2 Ironclad + 2 Shadowstep for hybrid bonuses)
- Some augments belong to **multiple sets** (flexible build options)

#### Space4X: Cybernetic/Bioware Augments

**Example Sets**:

| Set Name | Manufacturer | Augments in Set | Set Bonuses |
|----------|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| **Titan Series** | MegaCorp Industries | Titan Arm, Titan Leg, Titan Spine, Titan Heart | (2) +30 HP<br>(3) +40% strength<br>(4) Can survive vacuum for 10 minutes |
| **Ghost Protocol** | Shadow Ops Division | Ghost Optics, Ghost Limbs, Ghost Neural | (2) +50% evasion<br>(3) Invisible to scanners when still |
| **Overmind Link** | Collective Hive | Hive Brain, Hive Heart, Hive Nerves | (2) Shared vision with nearby allies<br>(3) Shared XP with squad |

**Cross-Limb Bonuses**:
- Some manufacturers grant bonuses for **any 2+ augments** from their line (not specific pieces)
- Encourages brand loyalty or mixing for diverse effects

### Parameters and Variables

| Parameter | Default Value | Range | Effect |
|-----------|--------------|-------|--------|
| **Socket Count (Common Items)** | 1-2 | 0-6 | How many sockets normal items have |
| **Socket Count (Legendary Items)** | 3-4 | 1-6 | How many sockets rare items have |
| **Rune Drop Rate (Low-Tier)** | 10% | 1%-50% | Common rune drop chance |
| **Rune Drop Rate (High-Tier)** | 0.5% | 0.1%-5% | Legendary rune drop chance |
| **Enchantment Slots** | 3 | 1-5 | How many enchantments per item |
| **Potion Combo Window** | 5 seconds | 1-30s | Time window to drink potions for combo |
| **Augment Slots** | 4 (two arms, two legs) | 2-10 | How many augments entity can equip |
| **Set Bonus Thresholds** | 2, 3, 4 pieces | Varies | How many pieces needed for set bonuses |

### Edge Cases

- **Partial Runeword**: Inserting runes in wrong order = no runeword, only individual rune effects
- **Socket Limit**: Cannot insert more runes than item has sockets
- **Rune Removal**: Removing rune destroys runeword, rune may be lost (or reclaimed with special tool)
- **Enchantment Overlap**: If two synergies share same enchantment, only one synergy active (player chooses)
- **Potion Combo Failure**: Drinking potions too slowly = no combo, just individual effects
- **Augment Rejection**: Low-quality augments may fail to install, damage entity (risk vs reward)
- **Set Bonus Breaking**: Replacing 1 set piece breaks set bonus immediately

## Player Interaction

### Player Decisions

- **Runeword Planning**: Hunt specific runes to complete planned runeword
- **Enchantment Synergy**: Choose enchantments that synergize vs. max individual stats
- **Potion Pre-Prep**: Carry combo potions or brew on-demand?
- **Augment Investment**: Commit to full set for max bonuses or mix sets for flexibility?
- **Build Diversity**: Create specialized builds (glass cannon, tank, support) via combos

### Skill Expression

- **Theorycrafting**: Discovering hidden synergies, optimal runeword builds
- **Resource Management**: Hoarding rare runes for perfect item vs. using on "good enough" item
- **Adaptive Builds**: Swapping enchantments/augments mid-campaign to counter enemies
- **Combo Execution**: Timing potion combos mid-combat without wasting effects

### Feedback to Player

- **Visual feedback**:
  - Socketed runes glow in item icon
  - Runeword activation shows special visual effect (aura, glow)
  - Synergy enchantments pulse when active
  - Set bonuses display on character sheet with checkmarks
- **Numerical feedback**:
  - Stat sheet shows base stats + runeword bonuses + synergy bonuses + set bonuses (broken down)
  - Combo effects appear as temporary buffs with icons
  - Enchantment synergy tooltip explains bonus
- **Audio feedback**:
  - Runeword activation sound (satisfying "click" or chime)
  - Potion combo fanfare (special sound when combo triggers)
  - Set bonus completion sound

## Balance and Tuning

### Balance Goals

1. **Combo Viability**: Synergies should be competitive with raw stat stacking
2. **Build Diversity**: Multiple viable builds, not one "best" combo
3. **Progression**: Low-tier combos accessible early, high-tier combos endgame goals
4. **Discovery Joy**: Finding new synergies should feel rewarding

### Tuning Knobs

1. **Runeword Power**: How much stronger runewords are vs. individual runes
2. **Synergy Magnitude**: How impactful enchantment synergies are
3. **Set Bonus Scaling**: Linear vs. exponential set bonuses (2-piece vs. 4-piece power jump)
4. **Combo Duration**: How long consumable combos last (balance vs. cost)
5. **Rarity of Components**: How hard it is to complete high-end combos

### Known Issues

- **Meta Dominance**: If one runeword/combo too strong, becomes mandatory
- **Rune Hoarding**: Players hoard rare runes, never use them (fear of wasting)
- **Combo Complexity**: Too many combos = overwhelming, hard to remember
- **Set Inflexibility**: Full-set requirement locks players into single build path
- **Potion Spam**: If combo potions too strong, combat becomes potion-chugging simulator

## Implementation Notes

### Technical Approach

**Runeword System**:
```csharp
// Socketed Item
public struct SocketedItem : IComponentData {
    public int SocketCount;
    public FixedList512Bytes<RuneID> InsertedRunes; // Ordered list
    public RunewordID ActiveRuneword; // If valid combo
}

// Runeword Definition
public struct RunewordDef {
    public FixedString64Bytes Name;
    public FixedList128Bytes<RuneID> RequiredRunes; // Exact order
    public ItemType ValidItemTypes; // Weapon, Armor, Shield, etc.
    public DynamicBuffer<StatModifier> Bonuses;
}

// Runeword Activation System
public partial class RunewordDetectionSystem : SystemBase {
    protected override void OnUpdate() {
        // Check all socketed items for valid runeword patterns
        Entities.ForEach((ref SocketedItem item) => {
            RunewordID detected = DetectRuneword(item.InsertedRunes);
            if (detected != RunewordID.None) {
                item.ActiveRuneword = detected;
                ApplyRunewordBonuses(item.Entity, detected);
            }
        }).ScheduleParallel();
    }
}
```

**Enchantment Synergy System**:
```csharp
// Enchanted Item
public struct EnchantedItem : IComponentData {
    public FixedList128Bytes<EnchantmentID> Enchantments;
    public FixedList128Bytes<SynergyID> ActiveSynergies;
}

// Synergy Definition
public struct SynergyDef {
    public FixedList128Bytes<EnchantmentID> RequiredEnchantments;
    public SynergyEffect Effect;
}

// Synergy Detection System
public partial class EnchantmentSynergySystem : SystemBase {
    protected override void OnUpdate() {
        Entities.ForEach((ref EnchantedItem item) => {
            item.ActiveSynergies = DetectSynergies(item.Enchantments);
            ApplySynergyEffects(item.Entity, item.ActiveSynergies);
        }).ScheduleParallel();
    }
}
```

**Consumable Combo System**:
```csharp
// Consumable Combo Tracker
public struct ConsumableComboTracker : IComponentData {
    public FixedList128Bytes<PotionID> RecentConsumables;
    public double LastConsumeTime;
    public float ComboWindow; // 5 seconds
}

// Combo Detection System
public partial class ConsumableComboSystem : SystemBase {
    protected override void OnUpdate() {
        double currentTime = SystemAPI.Time.ElapsedTime;
        Entities.ForEach((ref ConsumableComboTracker tracker) => {
            if (currentTime - tracker.LastConsumeTime > tracker.ComboWindow) {
                // Clear recent consumables (window expired)
                tracker.RecentConsumables.Clear();
            }
            // When new consumable taken, check for combo
            ComboID combo = DetectCombo(tracker.RecentConsumables);
            if (combo != ComboID.None) {
                ApplyComboEffect(tracker.Entity, combo);
                tracker.RecentConsumables.Clear(); // Combo consumed
            }
        }).ScheduleParallel();
    }
}
```

**Set Bonus System**:
```csharp
// Augment Inventory
public struct AugmentInventory : IBufferElementData {
    public AugmentID Augment;
    public SetID Set; // Which set this augment belongs to
}

// Set Bonus Tracker
public struct SetBonusTracker : IComponentData {
    public FixedList128Bytes<SetID> ActiveSets;
    public FixedList128Bytes<int> SetPieceCounts; // How many pieces of each set
}

// Set Bonus System
public partial class SetBonusCalculationSystem : SystemBase {
    protected override void OnUpdate() {
        Entities.ForEach((Entity e, ref SetBonusTracker tracker, in DynamicBuffer<AugmentInventory> augments) => {
            // Count pieces per set
            tracker.SetPieceCounts.Clear();
            foreach (var augment in augments) {
                IncrementSetCount(tracker, augment.Set);
            }
            // Apply set bonuses based on piece counts
            ApplySetBonuses(e, tracker);
        }).ScheduleParallel();
    }
}
```

### Godgame-Specific: Rune Discovery

- Runes can be found in:
  - Dungeon loot
  - Floating island treasure
  - Crafted by master smiths (expensive)
  - Quest rewards
  - Blood moon drops (high-tier runes)

### Space4X-Specific: Core Salvage

- Cores salvaged from:
  - Derelict ships
  - Anomalous rifts
  - Enemy wreckage
  - Research projects
  - Alien megastructure loot

### Performance Considerations

- **Combo Detection**: Only check combos when item changes or consumable taken (not every frame)
- **Synergy Caching**: Cache detected synergies, recalculate only when enchantments change
- **Set Bonus Updates**: Batch set bonus applications, avoid per-augment recalculation
- **Database Lookups**: Runeword/synergy definitions in static databases, fast lookups

### Testing Strategy

1. **Unit tests for**:
   - Runeword pattern matching (correct order detection)
   - Synergy detection (all combinations)
   - Potion combo timing (window mechanics)
   - Set bonus counting (partial sets, full sets)

2. **Playtests should verify**:
   - Combos feel rewarding and discoverable
   - Build diversity exists (not one dominant build)
   - Progression satisfying (early combos → endgame combos)
   - Combo tooltips clear and helpful

3. **Balance tests should measure**:
   - Power spike from runewords vs. base items
   - Synergy impact on combat effectiveness
   - Set bonus value vs. mixed builds
   - Rune acquisition rate (time to complete runeword)

## Examples

### Example Scenario 1: Completing "Steel" Runeword (Godgame)

**Setup**: Warrior has 3-socket sword (no runes yet). Has collected **Tir** and **El** runes, hunting for **Eth**.
**Action**:
- Warrior clears dungeon, defeats boss
- Boss drops **Eth** rune (lucky!)
- Warrior returns to village, visits blacksmith
- Inserts runes in order: Tir → El → Eth
**Result**:
- **"Steel"** runeword activates
- Sword gains:
  - Base damage: 50
  - Tir bonus: +5 damage
  - El bonus: +10 attack speed
  - Eth bonus: +5 damage
  - **Steel runeword bonus**: +30% damage (+15), +25% attack speed
- Final stats: 70 damage, +35% attack speed
- **Consequence**: Warrior becomes significantly stronger, ready for harder content

### Example Scenario 2: Enchantment Synergy Discovery (Godgame)

**Setup**: Mage has staff with 3 enchantment slots. Applies **Flame** and **Molten** enchantments.
**Action**:
- Mage tests staff in combat
- Kills enemy with fire spell
- **Surprise**: Enemy explodes in fiery burst, damaging nearby enemies!
- Mage realizes **Flame + Molten = Inferno synergy** (discovered!)
**Result**:
- Synergy tooltip appears: "Inferno: Enemies killed by fire explode, spreading flames"
- Mage adjusts tactics, groups enemies for explosion chains
- **Consequence**: Mage builds entire strategy around synergy (AoE focus)

### Example Scenario 3: Tri-Resist Potion Combo (Godgame)

**Setup**: Adventurer facing elemental boss (uses fire, cold, lightning attacks). Carries 3 resist potions.
**Action**:
- Boss fight begins, adventurer takes massive elemental damage
- Adventurer chugs **Fire Resist + Cold Resist + Lightning Resist** potions in rapid succession (within 3 seconds)
- **Tri-Resist** combo activates!
**Result**:
- Base potion effects: +50% fire resist, +50% cold resist, +50% lightning resist
- **Tri-Resist combo**: Immune to all elemental damage for 30 seconds
- Adventurer tanks boss attacks, deals damage without fear
- Boss defeated
- **Consequence**: Adventurer learns combo, brews Tri-Resist potions for future elemental encounters

### Example Scenario 4: Titan Series Set Bonus (Space4X)

**Setup**: Ace pilot has 2 Titan augments (Titan Arm, Titan Leg). Seeking Titan Spine to complete 3-piece bonus.
**Action**:
- Pilot raids derelict ship, salvages **Titan Spine** augment
- Returns to station, installs Titan Spine
**Result**:
- 2-piece bonus (already had): +30 HP
- **3-piece bonus (NEW)**: +40% strength
- Pilot can now:
  - Lift heavier objects (boarding action advantage)
  - Melee attacks deal massive damage
  - Can operate heavy weapons solo
- **Consequence**: Pilot becomes elite boarding specialist, leads assault teams

### Example Scenario 5: Trinity Cannon Core Sequence (Space4X)

**Setup**: Flagship has 3-socket railgun module. Has **Flux Core** and **Plasma Core**, seeking second **Flux Core**.
**Action**:
- Fleet raids alien fortress, loots **Flux Core**
- Returns to base, installs cores in sequence: Flux → Plasma → Flux
- **Trinity Cannon** sequence activates!
**Result**:
- Base railgun: 100 damage, single shot
- Core bonuses:
  - Flux: +10 damage
  - Plasma: +20 damage
  - Flux: +10 damage
- **Trinity Cannon sequence bonus**: +50% damage, fires 3-round burst
- Final stats: 210 damage per shot, 3 shots per attack = **630 total damage**
- **Consequence**: Flagship becomes anti-capital ship platform, one-shots enemy cruisers
